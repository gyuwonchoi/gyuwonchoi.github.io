I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Electrical Engineering at KAIST, advised by Professor Dae-Shik Kim. My research focuses on the interpretability, explainability, and safety of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs). I am particularly interested in understanding and improving the robustness of these models, with applications extending to Vision-Language-Action models (VLA) and autonomous driving systems.
